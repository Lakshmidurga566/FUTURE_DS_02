# Customer Churn Analysis Report

## Objective

To analyze customer churn behavior and identify key factors affecting customer retention.

## Dataset

Telco Customer Churn Dataset

## Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Power BI

## Dataset Overview

* Total Customers: 7043
* Total Columns: 21
* Churned Customers: 1869
* Churn Rate: 26.5%

## Key Findings

### Gender vs Churn

Gender has minimal impact on customer churn. Male and female customers show similar churn patterns.

### Contract Type vs Churn

Customers with Month-to-Month contracts have the highest churn rate. Customers with One-Year and Two-Year contracts are more likely to stay.

### Internet Service vs Churn

Fiber Optic customers exhibit the highest churn compared to DSL and customers without internet service.

### Payment Method vs Churn

Customers using Electronic Check have significantly higher churn rates than customers using automatic payment methods.

### Tenure Analysis

Customers who churn have an average tenure of about 18 months, while retained customers stay for about 38 months.

## Recommendations

1. Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts.
2. Improve retention strategies for Fiber Optic customers.
3. Promote automatic payment methods.
4. Focus on retaining new customers during their first two years.

## Conclusion

Customer churn is strongly influenced by contract type, internet service, payment method, and customer tenure. Targeted retention strategies can help reduce churn and improve customer loyalty.
